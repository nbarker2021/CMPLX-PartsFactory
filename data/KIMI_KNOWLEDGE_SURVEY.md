## 6. Gaps — what's not in Kimi's catalog yet

These are the things our prep work should still do, based on what isn't already populated above.

- **`canonicalization_v2.canonical_file` is empty** — Kimi has the artifact inventory and the cluster schema but hasn't yet produced canonicals at this layer. Our atomic merger output can fill this.
- **`yard_inventory.canonical_file` is nearly empty** — same story, the yard scan inventoried 55,140 files but only produced 1 canonical so far.
- **Localization layout** — Kimi catalogues *where* files are; what's missing is a **staging directory** that gathers the real-work files into a clean per-family layout suitable for git.
- **`.gitignore` draft** — the noise classifications in `yard_inventory.file_classification` give us the data; we still need to render them as gitignore rules.
- **Provenance manifest** — when we localize, we need a manifest mapping `staging/<family>/<file>.py` back to the list of `evidence_file.host_path` rows it consolidated.
